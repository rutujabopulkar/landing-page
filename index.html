The Reservations

For bookings,
We are open from Tuesday to Sunday from 7 pm for dinner,
and from 12:30 - 2:30 pm on Sundays for lunch.

The restaurant is closed on Mondays.
For reservations,
call between 12:00 pm to 8:00 pm on +91 1234567890,
WhatsApp us your enquiry,
or email bookings@sandhyarestaurant.com.

Our 10-course tasting menu is priced at Rs. 4500*.
cocktail pairings are priced at Rs. 7000
Our non-alcoholic pairing is priced at Rs. 1950*.

Please note we require an advance deposit to confirm all tables.

We offer seasonal tasting menus and encourage you to share any
allergies or dietary restrictions so we can tailor the menu to your
preference.

*Price excludes service charge and government taxes as applicable.
